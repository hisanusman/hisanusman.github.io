I am a final-year Bachelor of Science (Artificial Intelligence) undergraduate at the National University of Computer & Emerging Sciences (NUCES). My final year project, under the supervision of Dr. Usman Haider, focuses on detecting violent activities in CCTV footage using state-of-the-art multi-modal computer vision techniques. The project integrates large language models (LLMs) and artificial intelligence to automate emergency call handling and detailed report generation. Additionally, it incorporates advanced scene understanding and facial recognition technologies to identify potential criminals, contributing to a safer and more secure environment for all. My work reflects a deep understanding of AI systems and their real-world applications, particularly in Natural Language Processing, Generative AI, and Computer Vision.

My research interests encompass Natural Language Processing, Generative AI, Agentic AI, Machine Learning, and Computer Vision. I am passionate about pushing the boundaries of AI research and am actively seeking graduate roles/research positions to further contribute to this field.
